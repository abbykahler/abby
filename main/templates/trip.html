Trip Tracker

Trip Tracker is a simple app that that track your next trip. The landing page has a registration and login with valdiations to make sure the user can access their account.

The home page will display your itinerary after the trip is created.

The create page allows the user to customize their trip.

The travel plan is now updated on the home screen where the trip can be removed or edited.
